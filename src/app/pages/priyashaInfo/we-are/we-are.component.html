Our vision

Anamnesis has been conceptualized and built with an aim to provide seamless healthcare services to Indian diaspora, transforming how Indians store, track, transport individual health records digitally and keep in-sync with global health trends simultaneously. Our vision is to make Anamnesis a trusted household name in India when it comes to online healthcare services ranging from ordering healthcare products, booking a lab visit, doctor consultation to many more..

Our Mission

We all know personal Health is of paramount importance for us and for the society. But with increase of population and with increasing age of the population, the need and the way people would avail healthcare services requires transformation. We at Anamnesis believed that right mix of technology, data and accessibility of best-in-class services can address the transforming need of the population.

We will be continuously working on this path with a critical focus given to ease of use, secured data storage and anywhere anytime accessibility of the platform – “Anamnesis”.
